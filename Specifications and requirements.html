Specifications and requirements

Characteristics we look for

  • Problem-Solver
  • Time management
  • Organised
  • Attention to detail
  • Eagerness to learn

Qualifications we accept

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineer ing, Information Technology, or a related field. Associate's degree or diploma in web development or a related discipline may also be accepted, especially if coupled with relevant experience.
  • Work Experience as a web developer through internships, professional employment or programs.
  • Technical skills such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and related frameworks such as Angular or React etc.
  • Certifications in Oracle, AWS Developer, Microsoft, W3C or other institutions or Google.
  • Advanced degrees such as Master's in Computer Science or related fields are prefered for senior positions.
  • Further education in web development courses or related areas.

Who are we looking for?

Here at Hot Beans web development we strive for success in a forever developing technological environment, therefore we believe that we can give people a kickstart in their career or further their coding abilities through our employment. We only hire those who have good potential in the business and believe that they can make a difference or provide outstanding work.

Interviews and Applications may also provide a determination on the basis of your employment.
